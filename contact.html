Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my thesis or research?

You can submit your academic work through our online submission portal. Create an account, fill out the submission form, and upload your documents. Our review team will process your submission within 5-7 business days.

Is there a fee for accessing documents?

Basic access to our repository is free for all users. Premium features, including advanced search filters and detailed analytics, are available through our subscription plans.

How do I report copyright issues?

We take intellectual property seriously. If you believe content infringes on your copyright, please contact our legal team at legal@UniGradcenter.com with detailed information about the alleged infringement.
